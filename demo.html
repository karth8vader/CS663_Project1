Step 2 — Hand Detection & Landmarks

Normalize coordinates (translate, scale, rotate) and compute angles/distances as features.

Step 4 — Classification

Use an MLP/CNN for static signs and a temporal model (LSTM/TCN/Transformer) for dynamic signs.
